Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

A household favorite soup! A super easy recipe to make when you're lazy to cook or just feeling under the weather. It will warm you up from the inside out! You can get this recipe to the table in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • Onions and Celery
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth (Both can be used!)
  • Chicken
  • Noodles
  • Carrots
  • Basil and Oregano

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pot over medium and heat. Add onion and celery until tender, about 5 minutes
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
